How to Play & Rating System

  • Click the Start button to begin the aim training session.
  • Click on targets as quickly as possible when they appear.
  • Your reaction time is measured from when a target appears to when you click it.
  • Accuracy is calculated based on hits vs. misses.
  • Your rating is determined by your average reaction time and accuracy:
Elite: <150ms with >90% accuracy
Very Fast: 150-200ms with >80% accuracy
Fast: 200-300ms with >70% accuracy
Average: 300-500ms with >60% accuracy
Slow: >500ms or <60% accuracy
